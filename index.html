Why Restorations Matter

Safety, Aesthetics, and Vehicle Value

Don't compromise. Foggy headlights aren't just an eyesore—they are a safety hazard that depreciates your vehicle. Here is why clear headlights are crucial.

Restore Nighttime Safety

Oxidized, cloudy lenses block and scatter up to 80% of your headlight's output, creating a serious hazard for night driving. We restore your light output back to 100% clarity, significantly extending your beam distance so you can see road hazards and wildlife ahead of time.

Boost Vehicle Resale Value

First impressions matter. Weathered, yellowed headlights are the single quickest way to make a modern car look neglected and old. Restoring your headlights adds immediate curb appeal, making the entire car look years newer and adding hundreds of dollars in visual resale value.

2-Year UV Clearcoat Warranty

DIY cleaning kits use temporary chemical wipes that wear off in weeks. We seal our sanding and polishing process with a professional-grade, automotive-grade UV-resistant clearcoat. This creates a hard shield that protects the plastic from sunlight, guaranteed for at least 2 years.

How We Do It

Our 3-Step Precision Process

We use professional techniques and high-quality materials to deliver a factory-finish look that stands the test of time.

1

Prep & Protect

We thoroughly clean the headlights and apply heavy-duty automotive masking tape to safeguard the surrounding vehicle body paint before work begins.

2

Multi-Stage Wet Sanding

We sand away the damaged, oxidized outer plastic using sequential grits of premium sandpaper, progressing down to a ultra-fine finish to clear all micro-scratches.

3

UV Protection Coat

We apply a high-end UV clearcoat. This bonds directly with the freshly polished polycarbonate, providing deep optical clarity and shielding it from yellowing for 2+ years.
